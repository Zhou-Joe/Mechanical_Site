About FKM Specification

The FKM specification is a strength analysis standard published by the German Mechanical Engineering Research Association (FKM), widely used for fatigue and static strength assessment of mechanical components. This module implements the core calculation methods of the FKM specification, including:

  • Material fatigue limit calculation
  • Size effect correction
  • Surface roughness correction
  • Mean stress sensitivity analysis
  • Safety factor determination
  • Damage accumulation assessment
